AFL

AFL is a security-oriented fuzzer that uses compiler instrumentation to discover crashes in software programs.

ocamlopt-afl allows us to fuzz OCaml programs, and was merged into trunk for OCaml 4.05.

ocaml-afl-persistent allows you to fuzz things really fast!
